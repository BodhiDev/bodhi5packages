Quick Launcher


Quick Launcher is a Moksha module which, as its name implies, allows you to find and open things on your computer. Want to get to the Favorite Applications dialog box to set up your Favorites Menu? Then just type fav into Everything and hit Enter. Want to launch Terminology? Then enter into the Quick Launcher. This brief guide can only describe the basic features of the launcher; for more information follow the links on the Resources page at the end of this QuickStart Guide.

You can open the Quick Launcher by clicking its gadget on the shelf, or by pressing the key bindings Alt+Esc or Ctrl+Alt+Space, or from Main Menu > Quick Launcher. It will close automatically after performing the requested action, otherwise close it by pressing Esc or clicking outside its window.
